01
Retail & FMCG · Power BI Dashboard Build

From 5 disconnected spreadsheets to one executive dashboard

A mid-sized South African retail group ran sales, stock, HR, finance, and returns reporting across five separate Excel files — maintained by different departments, updated at different times. Leadership spent three days each month manually consolidating before any decision could be made.

The Approach

Applied CRISP-DM from business understanding upward. Mapped all five sources, identified conflicts, designed a unified Power BI data model with a star schema, and built a single executive dashboard with real-time drill-down by store, region, and product category.

02
Financial Services · Data Quality Audit + Excel Model

Uncovering R2M in revenue leakage through a data quality audit

A Gauteng-based financial services firm suspected their month-end reconciliation numbers were unreliable. Their Excel revenue model had been built incrementally over four years by multiple people — full of hardcoded values, broken formulas, and undocumented assumptions. Senior leadership had lost confidence in the numbers.

The Approach

Conducted a structured data quality audit across completeness, accuracy, consistency, and timeliness dimensions. Traced every formula dependency, documented all data flows, identified R2M+ in billing mismatches. Rebuilt the revenue model from scratch with full auditability and scenario modelling.

R2M
In revenue leakage identified and remediated within 6 weeks
3d→2h
Month-end close time reduced from 3 days to under 2 hours
100%
Auditability — every number traceable to source

03
Healthcare · Data Strategy & Governance Framework

Building the data foundation that enabled a first BI layer

A private healthcare group with three facilities had years of patient, billing, and operational data in siloed systems with no governance, no defined ownership, and no POPIA-aligned handling processes. They wanted dashboards — but the foundation wasn't there yet.

The Approach

Advised against jumping straight to dashboards. Instead delivered a 12-week data strategy and governance engagement: maturity assessment, stakeholder workshops, formal governance charter, data ownership model, POPIA compliance alignment, and a phased 18-month roadmap. Only then did we scope the BI layer.

Phased data roadmap — governance to live BI in three stages
