Retail & FMCG · Power BI Dashboard Build

From 5 disconnected spreadsheets to one executive dashboard

A mid-sized South African retail group was running sales, stock, HR, finance, and returns reporting across five separate Excel files — maintained by different departments, updated at different times, with no single version of the truth. Leadership spent three days each month manually consolidating reports before any decision could be made.

01
The Approach

Applied CRISP-DM from business understanding upward. Mapped all five data sources, identified overlap and conflicts, designed a unified data model in Power BI with a star schema, and built a single executive dashboard with real-time drill-down by store, region, and product category.

70%
Reduction in monthly reporting time — from 3 days to under 9 hours
5→1
Data sources unified into a single source of truth
R2M+
In previously invisible stock write-offs identified in the first month post-launch

Financial Services · Data Quality Audit + Excel Model

Uncovering R2M in revenue leakage through a data quality audit

A Gauteng-based financial services firm suspected their month-end reconciliation numbers were unreliable but couldn't pinpoint why. Their Excel-based revenue model had been built incrementally over four years by multiple people — full of hardcoded values, broken formulas, and undocumented assumptions. Senior leadership had lost confidence in the numbers.

02
The Approach

Conducted a structured data quality audit across completeness, accuracy, consistency, and timeliness dimensions. Traced every formula dependency, documented all data flows, identified R2M+ in billing mismatches and unreconciled entries. Rebuilt the revenue model from scratch with full auditability, version control, and scenario modelling.

R2M
In revenue leakage identified and remediated within the first 6 weeks
3d→2h
Month-end close time reduced from 3 days to under 2 hours
100%
Auditability — every number traceable to source with full documentation

Healthcare · Data Strategy & Governance Framework

Building the data foundation that enabled a first BI layer

A private healthcare group with three facilities had years of patient, billing, and operational data sitting in siloed systems — an ageing practice management system, a separate billing platform, and spreadsheet-based reporting. They wanted analytics and dashboards, but had no data governance, no defined ownership, and no POPIA-aligned data handling process. Analytics wasn't the problem — the foundation was.

03
The Approach

Advised against jumping to dashboards immediately. Instead delivered a 12-week data strategy and governance engagement: data maturity assessment, stakeholder workshops, a formal governance charter, data ownership and stewardship model, POPIA compliance alignment, and a phased 18-month roadmap. Only once the foundation was solid did we scope the BI layer.

18mo
Phased data roadmap delivered — from governance to live BI in three stages
POPIA
Full data handling alignment achieved across all three facilities
1st
Functioning BI layer — built on a trusted, governed data foundation for the first time
